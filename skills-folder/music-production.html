Music showroom

Made with Ableton Live. And love.

This video was produced for the HBO West World scoring competition, and all of the music was composed by me.
From the beginning until timestamp 0:34, we have some taiko drums playing with a big and dark horn sound. Then, beginning at 0:34, I laid out snare sounds and a very dreamy synth to try to recreate the character's feelings.
The main part begins at 1:04, with a futuristic house beat inspired by Daft Punk's 'Derezzed.'
Finally, at 3:56, you can hear my sound design for the West World logo transform into the HBO logo.

These next songs were created solely for entertainment purposes.
